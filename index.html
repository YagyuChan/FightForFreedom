About the Game

Welcome to the official website for Fight For Freedom! This game takes place across 3 different timelines with 3 different protagonists (Killjoy Nagasaka, Melfina Sayana and Rin Yomikaze) on their adventures to fight the Lavaspawn threat in the past, present and future.

Story

Step into the shoes of the Lavaspawn slayers and enter a turn based adventure across Akina Sara in 3 different perspectives with constant updates to the experience and story. Killjoy Nagasaka adventures over modern Akina Sara seeking out her friends and meeting new ones along the way while Melfina adventures past Akina Sara after a tragic event that forces her to flee into the past and Rin Yomikaze adventures in the future in an effort to counter an old threat thought to be destroyed.

Key Features

  • Turn Based Combat: Experience a turn based adventure across unique regions.
  • Character Progression: Connect your progress through our specialized progression systems.
  • Evolving World: Explore new regions, NPCs, Characters, Classes, Outfits, Quests and more added in every update.
